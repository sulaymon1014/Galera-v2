House rules

Short, and
seriously kept.

Our moderators welcome new members, spark conversation, and enforce these rules consistently — first with a word, then a warning, then a pause, and in rare cases a goodbye. Report anything with the flag on every post.

  1. Critique the work, never the person. “This isn’t resolved” travels; “you aren’t” doesn’t.
  2. Credit every artist whose work you post. Reposting without credit is a one-warning offence.
  3. No AI-generated work presented as hand-made. Tools are welcome; deception is not.
  4. Commission offers stay in the Commissions section. Enthusiasm is welcome; ambush is not.
  5. Supporter-only files stay supporter-only. Sharing them out is theft from a working artist.
